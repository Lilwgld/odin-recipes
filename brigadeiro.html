Brigadeiro

Brigadeiro is a very popular dessert in Brazil. It is sold everywhere, whether on the streets or in expensive coffee-shops, because this chocolate truffle has many combinations. However, it is more consumed as chocolate flavor.

Like the mousse, it also has a practical and quick recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted margarine
  • 7 tablespoons of chocolate milk
  • 1 bag of chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. In a deep pan, place the condensed milk, margarine and chocolate milk
  2. Cook over medium heat until the brigadeiro start to unglue from the pan
  3. Let it cool and then make small balls of brigadeiro with your hands
  4. Roll the small balls in the chocolate sprinkles
