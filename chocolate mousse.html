Chocolate Mousse

Chocolate mousse is a great option to eat delicious and easy-to-prepare food. You just need three ingredients and an hour and a half to make it. By the way, it's very popular.

This dessert is usually consumed in a beautiful glass bowl with the help of a spoon and is often sold in cake shop.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of table cream
  • 8.8 ounces of sliced chocolate
  • 1/2 can of condensed milk

Steps

  1. Melt your chocolate
  2. Mix all the ingredients until it turns into a cream
  3. Place the cream in the freezer for 20 minutes
  4. Use the mixer in the cream for 3 minutes
  5. Place the cream in the freezer for 2 hours
