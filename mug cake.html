Mug Cake

Mug cake is the perfect option for those who can't waste time in the kitchen. It is a simple recipe, but also delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate powder
  • 3 heaping tablespoons of sugar
  • 4 heaping tablespoons wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 4 tablespoons of milk

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a large mug.
  2. Mix the ingredients
  3. Take to microwave for 3 minutes
